Notes
-----
- All SVG files use standard XML/SVG markup with no embedded scripts.
- Colour palettes are adapted from the EduQuest brand guidelines defined in
  shared/styles/variables-v2.css and shared/utils/pet-sprites.js.
- Character designs are inspired by real-world animals (echidna, sea turtle,
  primate) and are entirely original compositions for this project.
- For academic integrity purposes: no AI image generation services were used.
  All shapes, gradients, and paths were hand-authored in SVG markup.
