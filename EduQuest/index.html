About EduQuest

Gamified Learning for Every Learner

EduQuest is a gamified learning platform designed for students with ADHD and comorbid conditions. The clean, soothing interface avoids cognitive overload while delivering engaging, personalized learning experiences.

From the moment a user arrives, they choose their role and follow a path built for them:

  • Teachers access Classroom & IEP Management — create student profiles, set individualized goals, assign quizzes, and track progress with real-time analytics.
  • Students enter a Gamified Learning Experience — earn XP, hatch and evolve a companion egg, join elemental teams (Fire, Water, Grass), complete quests, and unlock achievements.
  • The warm parchment palette, clear navigation, and ADHD-friendly layouts ensure every learner can focus and thrive.
  • Join Now and begin your quest in minutes — completely free.

EduQuest’s mission: make every learner’s journey visible, celebrated, and worth continuing.
